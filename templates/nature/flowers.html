Scientific Name: Gloriosa superba

Zimbabwe's national flower, known for its striking red and yellow petals that curve backward like flames.

Flowering Seasons in Zimbabwe

Wet Season (Nov-Mar)
  • Peak flowering period for most species
  • Flame lilies bloom after first rains
  • Abundant wildflower displays
Dry Season (Apr-Oct)
  • Some drought-adapted species flower
  • Seed collection time
  • Preparation for next growing season
