Grasses of Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe's grasslands are the foundation of our ecosystems, supporting wildlife and agriculture across the country.

Red Grass

Scientific Name: Themeda triandra

A dominant grass species in Zimbabwe's highveld, known for its reddish color and high palatability to livestock.

Spear Grass

Scientific Name: Heteropogon contortus

Recognizable by its twisted awns, this grass is well-adapted to poor soils and dry conditions.

Learn More
Elephant Grass

Scientific Name: Pennisetum purpureum

A tall, robust grass that can reach 4 meters in height, important for fodder and erosion control.
