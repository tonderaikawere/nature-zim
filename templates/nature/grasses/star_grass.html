Star Grass

Cynodon plectostachyus

Star grass gets its name from its distinctive star-shaped seed heads. This creeping perennial grass is excellent for pastures and is widely used in Zimbabwe's agricultural systems due to its high nutritional value and palatability.

Features & Benefits

  • Growth habit: Creeping stoloniferous perennial
  • Seed heads: Distinctive star-shaped arrangement
  • Palatability: Excellent for livestock
  • Drought tolerance: Good resistance to dry conditions
  • Soil binding: Helps prevent erosion
Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Cynodon plectostachyus

Family: Poaceae

Growth form: Creeping perennial

Best use: Pasture grass
