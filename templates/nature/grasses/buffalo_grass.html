Buffalo Grass

Panicum maximum

Buffalo grass is a robust, tufted perennial grass that forms an important component of Zimbabwe's natural grasslands. It's highly valued for its palatability and nutritional value for livestock.

Characteristics

  • Height: 1-2 meters
  • Growth form: Robust tufted perennial
  • Palatability: Highly palatable to livestock
  • Nutritional value: High protein content
  • Habitat: Various soil types
Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Panicum maximum

Family: Poaceae

Origin: Tropical Africa

Uses: Grazing, hay production
