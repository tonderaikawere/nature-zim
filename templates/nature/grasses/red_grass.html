Red Grass

Themeda triandra

Red grass is one of Zimbabwe's most important and widespread grass species, dominating the highveld grasslands. Known for its distinctive reddish-brown color when mature, this perennial grass is highly valued for livestock grazing and plays a crucial role in the ecosystem.

Characteristics

  • Height: 30-120 cm
  • Color: Green when young, reddish-brown when mature
  • Growth: Perennial tufted grass
  • Habitat: Well-drained soils, highveld areas
  • Palatability: Highly palatable to livestock
Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Themeda triandra

Family: Poaceae

Distribution: Throughout Zimbabwe

Uses: Grazing, thatching, erosion control
