Elephant Grass

Pennisetum purpureum

Elephant grass is a tall, robust perennial grass that can reach heights of up to 4 meters. It's an important fodder crop and is also used for erosion control and biomass production in Zimbabwe.

Characteristics & Uses

  • Height: 2-4 meters
  • Growth: Fast-growing perennial
  • Uses: Livestock fodder, erosion control, biomass
  • Habitat: Moist, fertile soils
  • Yield: High biomass production
Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Pennisetum purpureum

Family: Poaceae

Origin: Tropical Africa

Growth Rate: Very fast
