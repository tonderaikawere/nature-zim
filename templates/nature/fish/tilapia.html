Tilapia

Oreochromis species

Tilapia are among Zimbabwe's most important freshwater fish, both ecologically and economically. These hardy cichlids are widely farmed and form the backbone of the country's aquaculture industry.

Characteristics

  • Size: 15-35 cm, depending on species
  • Habitat: Shallow waters, dams, rivers
  • Diet: Omnivorous - algae, plants, small animals
  • Breeding: Mouth-brooding behavior
  • Adaptability: Tolerates various water conditions
Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Oreochromis species

Family: Cichlidae

Economic importance: Major aquaculture species

Nutrition: High protein, low fat
