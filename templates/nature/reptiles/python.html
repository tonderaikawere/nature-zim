African Python

Python sebae

The African Python is one of the largest snakes in Africa and Zimbabwe's most impressive serpent. These non-venomous constrictors play an important role in controlling rodent populations and are revered in traditional culture.

Characteristics

  • Length: Up to 6 meters (average 3-4 meters)
  • Weight: Can exceed 50 kg
  • Hunting method: Constriction
  • Diet: Birds, mammals, occasionally reptiles
  • Habitat: Woodlands, grasslands, rocky areas
Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Python sebae

Family: Pythonidae

Status: Near Threatened

Reproduction: Lays 20-100 eggs

Cultural significance: Sacred in many traditions
