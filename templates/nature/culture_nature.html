Culture & Nature in Zimbabwe

The deep connections between Zimbabwean culture and the natural world

Traditional Ecological Knowledge

Zimbabwe's indigenous communities have developed sophisticated understanding of local ecosystems over thousands of years. This traditional knowledge continues to play important roles in conservation and sustainable resource management.
