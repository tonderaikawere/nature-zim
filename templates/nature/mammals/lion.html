African Lion

Panthera leo

The African Lion, known as the "King of Beasts," is one of Zimbabwe's most iconic predators. These magnificent cats are found in national parks and conservancies, playing a crucial role as apex predators in the ecosystem.

Characteristics

  • Size: Males 150-250 kg, females 120-180 kg
  • Mane: Males develop distinctive manes
  • Social structure: Live in prides of 10-15 individuals
  • Hunting: Primarily done by females
  • Territory: Prides control large territories
Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Panthera leo

Family: Felidae

Status: Vulnerable

Population: ~500-700 in Zimbabwe

Main Parks: Hwange, Mana Pools
