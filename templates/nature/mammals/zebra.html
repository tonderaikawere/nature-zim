Plains Zebra

Equus quagga

The Plains Zebra is one of Zimbabwe's most recognizable animals with its distinctive black and white stripes. These social herbivores form large herds and are an important part of the savanna ecosystem.

Characteristics

  • Stripes: Unique pattern for each individual
  • Size: 200-300 kg, 1.2-1.4 m at shoulder
  • Social behavior: Live in family groups and herds
  • Diet: Primarily grasses
  • Migration: Seasonal movements following rains
Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Equus quagga

Family: Equidae

Status: Near Threatened

Lifespan: 20-25 years

Predators: Lions, hyenas, wild dogs
