Sunflower

Helianthus annuus

Sunflowers are cultivated throughout Zimbabwe for their seeds and oil. These bright, cheerful flowers are known for their ability to track the sun across the sky and are an important agricultural crop.

Features

  • Height: 1-3 meters tall
  • Flower head: Large, yellow petals with dark center
  • Heliotropism: Young flowers follow the sun
  • Seeds: Rich in oil and nutrients
  • Growing season: Summer crop in Zimbabwe
Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Helianthus annuus

Family: Asteraceae

Uses: Oil production, food, ornamental

Economic Value: Important cash crop
