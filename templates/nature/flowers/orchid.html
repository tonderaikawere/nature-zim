Wild Orchid

Various species

Zimbabwe is home to numerous wild orchid species, these delicate and exotic flowers can be found in forests, grasslands, and rocky outcrops. They are prized for their intricate beauty and unique adaptations.

Characteristics

  • Flowers: Complex, often colorful and fragrant
  • Habitat: Forests, grasslands, rocky areas
  • Growth: Terrestrial or epiphytic
  • Pollination: Specialized relationships with insects
  • Conservation: Many species are rare and protected
Quick Facts

Family: Orchidaceae

Species: Over 400 in Zimbabwe

Flowering: Mainly rainy season

Status: Many species endangered
