Birds of Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is home to over 670 bird species, from the majestic African Fish Eagle to colorful bee-eaters.

African Fish Eagle

Scientific Name: Haliaeetus vocifer

Zimbabwe's national bird, known for its distinctive call and impressive fishing skills along rivers and lakes.

Learn More
Hornbill

Scientific Name: Tockus species

Large birds with distinctive curved bills, important seed dispersers in Zimbabwe's woodlands.

Learn More
Weaver Bird

Scientific Name: Ploceus species

Master architects known for their intricate woven nests, often seen in colonies near water sources.

Bird Watching in Zimbabwe

Best Locations
  • Hwange National Park
  • Mana Pools National Park
  • Eastern Highlands
  • Lake Kariba
Best Times
  • Early morning (5-9 AM)
  • Late afternoon (4-6 PM)
  • Wet season (Nov-Mar) for migrants
  • Dry season for water birds
