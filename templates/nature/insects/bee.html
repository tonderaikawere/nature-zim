Bees

Various species

Bees are among Zimbabwe's most important insects, providing essential pollination services for both wild plants and agricultural crops. From honeybees to solitary species, they play crucial ecological and economic roles.

Importance

  • Pollination: Essential for plant reproduction
  • Honey production: Important economic activity
  • Crop yields: Increase agricultural productivity
  • Biodiversity: Support ecosystem health
  • Traditional uses: Honey, wax, medicine
Quick Facts

Order: Hymenoptera

Species: 200+ in Zimbabwe

Social structure: Varies by species

Products: Honey, wax, propolis
