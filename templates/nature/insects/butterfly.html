Butterflies

Various species

Zimbabwe is home to over 400 butterfly species, making it one of Africa's most diverse butterfly regions. These beautiful insects are important pollinators and indicators of ecosystem health.

Characteristics

  • Wings: Colorful scales create patterns and colors
  • Life cycle: Complete metamorphosis (egg, larva, pupa, adult)
  • Diet: Adults feed on nectar, larvae eat specific plants
  • Flight: Diurnal (active during day)
  • Habitat: Gardens, forests, grasslands
Quick Facts

Order: Lepidoptera

Species in Zimbabwe: 400+

Role: Pollinators

Threats: Habitat loss, climate change
