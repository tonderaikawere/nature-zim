Ants

Various species

Ants are among the most successful insects on Earth, with complex social structures and important ecological roles. Zimbabwe hosts numerous ant species, from tiny garden ants to large driver ants.

Social Structure

  • Colonies: Highly organized social groups
  • Castes: Queens, workers, soldiers
  • Communication: Chemical signals (pheromones)
  • Cooperation: Work together for colony success
  • Roles: Soil aeration, seed dispersal, pest control
Quick Facts

Order: Hymenoptera

Species: 100+ in Zimbabwe

Colony size: Hundreds to millions

Diet: Omnivorous
