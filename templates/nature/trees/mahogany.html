Mahogany Tree

Khaya anthotheca

The African Mahogany is a magnificent tree prized for its beautiful reddish-brown timber. It's one of Zimbabwe's most valuable indigenous trees, playing important roles in both natural ecosystems and human communities.

Features

  • Height: Up to 30 meters tall
  • Timber: Beautiful reddish-brown wood
  • Leaves: Large compound leaves
  • Flowers: Small white flowers in clusters
  • Fruit: Woody capsules that split to release winged seeds
Quick Facts

Scientific Name: Khaya anthotheca

Family: Meliaceae

Uses: High-quality furniture, construction

Status: Protected species
