Zimbabwe's Elephant Population Shows Positive Growth

Recent wildlife census data reveals encouraging news for Zimbabwe's elephant conservation efforts. The country's elephant population has shown steady growth, reaching over 84,000 individuals across national parks and conservancies.

This positive trend is attributed to enhanced anti-poaching measures, community conservation programs, and improved habitat management practices implemented over the past five years.
